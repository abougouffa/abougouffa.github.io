Biography

Abdelhak is a Ph.D. Student in Robotics at the SATIE lab of Paris-Saclay University. His research interests include mobile robotics, indoor and outdoor localization, simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM), embedded systems, artificial intelligence and a little of computer vision. He works also as a research and development engineer at ez-Wheel as part of his Ph.D. studies.

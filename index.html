About Me

I'm Abdelhak BOUGOUFFA, from Algeria, 25 years old, Master student (Distributed Computing Systems) at the University of Boumerdes (UMBB), I'm a former president of the University Computer Club (CIUB), I'm also a self-made programmer/developer.

In the 2012/2013 season, I joined the university computer club CIUB. At the Club, I taught Programming (Using C and Python), GNU/Linux, Free and Open Source culture... In the next two seasons, I was elected as President of the club.

Now, I'm a second year Master student at the University of Boumerdes, in the field of "Distributed Computing Systems", I contribute in some free and open source projects in my free time.

Biography

Abdelhak is a Ph.D. Student in Robotics at the SATIE lab of Paris-Saclay University. His research interests include mobile robotics, indoor and outdoor localization and SLAM, embedded systems and artificial intelligence. He works also as a research and development engineer at ez-Wheel as part of his Ph.D. studies.
